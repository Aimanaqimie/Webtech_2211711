About Us

A "RM1 Campaign" is a fundraising or donation initiative in which people are encouraged to contribute or donate one Malaysian Ringgit (RM1). The campaign aims to collect a large number of small contributions, which will have a significant impact when added together. The idea is that a small donation from many people can add up to a large fund.
